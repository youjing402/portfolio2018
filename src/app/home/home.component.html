Thanks for visiting! I'm a product designer pursuing M.S. Human-Computer Interaction Design at Indiana University Bloomington. Previously, I completed my bachelor's in Computer Science.

I love exploring connections between people, environment, business, cultures, technology, and everything within the product ecosystem. I believe that design is not only about making things pleasing on aesthetic or experience level: it's about diving into people's mind and uncovering possibilities to envision a better alternative future.

If you are interested in talking with me, please feel free to drop me an email at: youjing402@gmail.com

I came from Xiamen, a beautiful island located in Southeastern China. I entered National University of Singapore to study Computer Science and explore the beauty of technology.

During my undergrad years, I spent half a year in Europe to complete the exchange program. There, I developed more interests in HCI through completing design courses in Sweden, and decided to continue my study after graduation.

Now that I'm in US studying HCI design, I hope to bring viewpoints from my background, and to grow as a product designer in the IT industry.

I acquired technical skills from my undergrad CS study.

I love being "geeky" and enjoy crafting things. I always keep eyes on emerging technologies and how they can be designed to create new experience and services to better solve people's problems.
